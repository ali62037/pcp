About PCP - Pie Classes Patna

Shaping Young Minds Since 2021

Welcome to Pie Classes Patna

PCP - Pie Classes Patna, located in Danapur, is a renowned coaching center widely recognized as the best place to learn and offers a conducive environment for academic growth. Established in 2021, we have been providing quality education to students of classes 4-10 following CBSE and ICSE curriculum.

Our institute specializes in providing comprehensive guidance with a focus on conceptual understanding, regular assessments, and personalized attention to ensure every student achieves their full potential.
